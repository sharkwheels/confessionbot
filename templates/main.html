What is it?

A twitter bot that takes your confessions in the form of DMs and anonymously posts them to its timeline. DMs that are tweeted are immediately deleted from the DM history. You can visit @smlconfessional to check it out!

How To Use It

The DMs on this bot are open, which means anyone can message it.

Send your message with a ~ in front of it.

Confessions are text and emoji only. Any links, mentions, pics, etc will result in the message to being tweeted.

Why Make This?

I was a really big fan of some of the internet confessionals from the early internet, and just wanted to make a tiny twitter one. I feel sometimes you just need to get something off your chest, without it being tied to you, and without the flood of responses that comes from public social media. Yell into the void, and let it sift away. Its sometimes just something you need.
